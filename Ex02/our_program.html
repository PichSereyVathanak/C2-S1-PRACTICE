Cat School Programs

Welcome to the Cat School Programs page!
Here, we offer a variety of classes to help every cat reach their full potential. From basic skills to
advanced tricks, our programs are designed to be fun, engaging, and enriching for cats of all ages!

Here are the Program for you to choose for ur cat:

Course Name Detail Duration
Basic Cat Etiquette
  • Teach your cat the fundamentals of being a well-behaved feline.
  • Learn skills like using the litter box properly, meowing at the right time, and understanding human commands (only when it suits the cat, of course!).
 4 weeks
Advanced Hunting
  • Does your cat love to pounce? This course is perfect for honing those hunting instincts.
  • Training includes sneak attacks, toy mouse capture, and expert feather-chasing strategies.
 6 weeks
Yoga for Cats
  • Cats are natural yogis! In this class, we'll help your feline master the perfect stretch, from the classic "Downward Dog" to the "Upward Stretch."
  • Ideal for increasing flexibility and relaxation.
 6 weeks
Napping 101
  • Napping is serious business, and every cat needs to know how to find the best spots.
  • This course covers everything from choosing the coziest sunbeam to avoiding disturbances during naps.
 8 weeks
